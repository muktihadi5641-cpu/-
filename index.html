Topik 1

The Four Stages of Collapse

四個崩塌階段

01

Evolution Summary of Hydraulic Heave

水力隆起的演變

水力隆起不會馬上發生。先有小漏水，後來慢慢變大，最後整個結構都壞了。這個過程很難發現，而且速度越來越快。

English Meaning

Structural failure caused by hydraulic heave does not happen instantly — it is a chain reaction that begins with minor seepage and escalates into an unstoppable structural collapse. The process is inherently hidden and progressively accelerating.

Key Takeaway: From a tiny water-flow disturbance to irreversible structural rupture — hydraulic heave is a highly concealed and progressively accelerating process.
01
Stage 1 · 階段 1

Initial Disturbance

初始破壞 / 開始階段

水從牆的下面慢慢流進來。土還沒壞，可是問題已經開始了。

English

Water begins seeping in from beneath the wall. The soil is still intact, but problems have already begun.

02
Stage 2 · 階段 2

Backward Erosion

逆向侵蝕 / 逐漸破壞

水流變大了，開始把土帶走。土裡面慢慢出現小通道。

English

The water flow gets stronger and carries soil away. Small seepage tunnels gradually form inside the soil.

03
Stage 3 · 階段 3

Flow Channel

流動通道形成

土裡面形成一條大通道。水一直往上流，越來越多的土被帶走。

English

A large flow channel forms inside the soil. Water keeps flowing upward, and more and more soil is washed away.

04
Stage 4 · 階段 4

Liquefaction & Collapse

液化與崩塌

土變成泥，不能再支撐東西。牆倒了，整個結構完全壞掉。

English

The soil turns into mud and can no longer support anything. The wall falls, and the entire structure completely fails.

Topik 2

Four Foundation Pit Scenarios

四種基坑情境

02

Analyzing Uplift Scenarios

抗浮情境分析

為了解決抗浮的問題，不同的土和設計會產生不同的水壓。下面介紹工程師最常遇到的四種情況。

English Meaning

To address anti-uplift safety concerns, different geological conditions and engineering designs produce vastly different pressure distributions. The following four scenarios outline the most common combinations encountered by civil engineers in the field.

Analyzing Hydrostatic Uplift Scenarios across different site and design conditions.
Concrete Floor
G > A = SAFE ✓
01 Safe Scenario

Concrete Floor

情況一：混凝土地板

地下水往上推 (A)。土和結構的重量往下壓 (G)。如果 G > A，就很安全。

English Meaning

Groundwater pushes upward (A). The weight of soil and structure pushes downward (G). When G > A, the foundation pit is safe.

Resistance +
02 Uplift Resistance

Sealing Layer

情況二：防水封底層 — 阻斷水壓，提升抗浮

底下有一層防水的東西。水還是往上推，可是這層可以幫忙擋住。

English Meaning

A waterproof sealing layer is placed at the base. Water still pushes upward, but the sealing layer significantly improves the anti-uplift capacity.

Low Risk
03 Flow Reduction

Low-Permeable Layer

情況三：嵌入低滲透土層 — 降低流速，減少風險

基坑在一種水不容易通過的土裡。這樣水流變慢，浮力就變小了。

English Meaning

The foundation pit is embedded in a low-permeability soil layer. This layer slows the water flow, so the uplift force becomes smaller and the risk drops.

Danger ⚠
04 High-Risk Concentration

Impermeable Layer Below

情況四：底部不透水層 — 壓力集中，極易隆起

基坑底下有一層水不能通過的東西。水沒地方走，水壓都集中在底部。如果設計不好，底部就會隆起來。

English Meaning

An impermeable layer below the pit traps the groundwater — pressure concentrates at the base. With insufficient design, bottom heave (uplift failure) may occur.
